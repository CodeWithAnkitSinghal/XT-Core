Come see the fanatastic Coit Tower!

Did you know that coit tower is a monument to the firefighters of san francisco?It rises 210 feet over telegraph hill and provides stunning views of the city

Money for the tower was provided by Lillie Coit, aneccentric socialite who had deep connection to the city's firefighters.

If you want to visit, consider walking! Parking is limited at the tower, and it's often faster (and more acenic) to just walk
